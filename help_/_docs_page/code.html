Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a dataset?

expand_more
You can upload datasets by navigating to the 'Datasets' page and clicking the 'Upload' button. We support CSV, JSON, and Excel file formats.

What chart types are available?

expand_more
Our playground supports a wide variety of chart types, including bar charts, line charts, scatter plots, pie charts, and heatmaps. You can select the chart type that best suits your data visualization needs.

How can I use AI insights?

expand_more
After creating a chart, click on the 'AI Insights' button. Our AI will analyze your data and provide key observations, trends, and potential outliers to help you understand your data better.
