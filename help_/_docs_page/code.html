Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a dataset?

expand_more
You can upload datasets by navigating to the 'Datasets' page and clicking the 'Upload' button. We support CSV, JSON, and Excel file formats.

What chart types are available?

expand_more
Our playground supports a wide variety of chart types, including bar charts, line charts, scatter plots, pie charts, and heatmaps.

How can I use AI insights?

expand_more
After creating a chart, click on the 'AI Insights' button. Our AI will analyze your data and provide key observations.

What file size limits apply when uploading data?

expand_more
The maximum supported file size is 50MB. For larger datasets, consider splitting the file into smaller parts before uploading.

Can I edit or delete uploaded datasets?

expand_more
Yes. Go to the 'Datasets' page, where you can view, rename, delete, or replace uploaded files anytime.

How can I share charts with my team?

expand_more
After creating a chart, use the 'Export' option to save it as PNG or PDF, or generate a shareable link to send to others.

Are my files stored securely?

expand_more
Yes. All files are encrypted and stored securely. You can delete them at any time from your dashboard.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

expand_more
Yes! The interface is fully responsive, allowing you to upload data, create charts, and view insights on your phone or tablet.
